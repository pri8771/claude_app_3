# Marketing Plan PRD — Foldlight

**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Date:** 2026-06-27 | **Status:** Draft

---

## Executive Summary

Foldlight's marketing strategy centers on organic viral content, premium Apple positioning, and community-led growth. Our ASMR-quality fold animations are inherently shareable. Our world-restoration narrative creates emotional investment beyond typical mobile games. We do not compete on ad spend — we compete on quality and word-of-mouth.

**Marketing Budget Year 1:** $120,000
**Target Downloads Year 1:** 500,000
**Blended CAC Target:** ≤ $1.80

## Brand Identity

### Brand Values
- **Original:** First spatial folding puzzle on iOS
- **Beautiful:** Every pixel intentional, every animation satisfying
- **Honest:** No dark patterns, no pay-to-win
- **Playful:** Whimsical world, emotional story

### Visual Identity
- **Color Palette:** Deep blue-black + stained glass golds + crystal blues
- **App Icon:** Dark background, crystalline folded light beam
- **Tagline:** "Fold the board. Bend the rules."
- **Voice:** Warm, intelligent, confident

### Brand Pillars
1. **Craft** — We obsess over animation quality and feel
2. **Story** — The world restoration narrative creates meaning
3. **Respect** — We respect player time and wallets
4. **Wonder** — Every fold should feel magical

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience (Core)
- **Age:** 25–40
- **Gender:** All genders, slight female skew (puzzle games)
- **Location:** US, UK, Canada, Australia, Japan
- **Device:** iPhone 13+ (appreciates 120fps ProMotion)
- **Behavior:** Plays games during commute/before bed, values aesthetics
- **Psychographic:** Appreciates Monument Valley, Alto's Odyssey, The Room
- **Budget:** Will spend $5-15 on quality games

### Secondary Audience (Casual)
- **Age:** 18–25
- **Location:** Global
- **Behavior:** TikTok-first discovery, shares ASMR content
- **Budget:** Free-to-play first, occasional small IAP

### Tertiary Audience (Puzzle Enthusiasts)
- **Age:** 30–55
- **Behavior:** Active on r/puzzlegames, Hacker News
- **Budget:** Happy to pay premium for quality

## Competitive Positioning

### Market Position
Foldlight occupies the **Premium Indie Puzzle** segment: high-quality, low price, cult following.

### Differentiation vs Competitors
| Competitor | Weakness | Foldlight Advantage |
|------------|----------|---------------------|
| Candy Crush | No depth, no story | Spatial mechanics + narrative |
| Monument Valley | No progression system | World restoration + procedural levels |
| The Room | Horror aesthetic limits audience | Whimsical, broadly appealing |
| Puzzlecraft | Dated design | Modern AAA aesthetic |

### Unique Value Proposition
"The first puzzle game that feels like a meditation — beautiful, surprising, and endlessly deep."

## Channel Strategy

### TikTok (Primary Organic Channel)
**Goal:** 10,000 followers by Month 1, 500K video views by Month 3

**Content Types:**
1. Satisfying fold-solve videos (15-30s, silent, ASMR)
2. "Did you know?" mechanic explanations
3. World restoration before/after reveals
4. Behind-the-scenes dev content
5. "Impossible fold" challenge videos

**Posting Schedule:** Mon/Wed/Fri 7pm EST
**Success Metric:** 500K views = organic viral threshold

**Creator Partnerships (Month 6):**
- 5 gaming creators with 50K–500K followers
- Gifted codes + revenue share for top performers
- Budget: $5,000

### Instagram/Reels (Secondary Organic)
**Goal:** 2,000 followers by Month 3

**Content:**
- Repurpose TikTok videos + biome art carousels
- Behind-the-scenes design process
- World map reveals, new biome teasers
- Posting: 4x/week (Tue/Thu/Sat + TikTok reposts)

### Reddit (Community Channel)
**Subreddits:** r/puzzlegames (200K+), r/indiegaming (700K+), r/iosgaming (500K+)

**Content:**
- Mechanic explanation posts ("We built a spatial folding puzzle engine — AMA")
- Weekly puzzle screenshot thread
- Beta feedback threads
- No spam — genuine community participation

### YouTube (Long-Form)
**Content:**
- 3-5 minute "How We Built Foldlight" dev diaries
- Puzzle design philosophy videos
- Speedrun showcase videos
- Posting: 2x/month

### Press & Media

**Tier 2 Targets:**

## Launch Campaign

### Pre-Launch (Months 1-6)
**Month 1-2:** Stealth build
- TikTok account created, 2x/week ASMR teasers
- Discord server launched
- Email list building (beta signup page)

**Month 3-4:** Beta announcement
- "We're making something magical" posts
- Beta signup CTA in all content
- Target 500 beta testers

**Month 5-6:** Launch countdown
- Press kit sent to 20 journalists
- Beta feedback incorporated
- App Store page live (pre-order if possible)
- Discord hits 1,000 members

### Launch Week (Month 7)
**Day -1:** Press embargo lifts at 9am EST
**Day 0:** App Store launch at 12am PST
- Twitter/X announcement thread
- TikTok "it's live" video
- Discord alert
- Email blast to waitlist

**Day 1-7:**
- Respond to every review (App Store)
- Monitor and reply to all social mentions
- Creator content goes live (5 partners)
- Submit "New Games We Love" Apple editorial pitch

### Post-Launch (Months 8+)
**Month 8:** First content update (new biome teaser)
**Month 9:** Seasonal event (Summer Solstice biome)
**Month 12:** Localization launch (JP, KR, DE, FR)
**Month 15:** Android assessment
