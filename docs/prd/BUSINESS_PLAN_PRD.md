# Business Plan PRD — Foldlight: Infinite Folding Puzzle

**Document Version:** 1.0.0
**Created:** 2026-06-27
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** Business / Product Lead

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Foldlight is a premium iOS puzzle game targeting the $8.9B casual mobile gaming market. It introduces a genuinely new mechanic (board folding) into the saturated puzzle genre, positioning it for organic discovery, Apple feature potential, and strong word-of-mouth.

**Mission:** Build a beloved puzzle game brand that generates sustainable revenue through ethical, player-first monetization.

**Target Launch:** Q4 2026
**Target Market:** iOS casual puzzle players, ages 22–45, English-speaking markets (US, UK, CA, AU)
**Business Model:** Free-to-play with IAP (cosmetics, consumables, subscription)
**Revenue Goal (Year 1):** $35,000–$175,000 (base to upside)

---

## 2. Market Opportunity

### 2.1 Market Size

- Global mobile gaming market: $92B (2025)
- Casual/puzzle segment: ~$8.9B (2025)
- iOS casual puzzle games top charts consistently
- Top-grossing puzzle games (Block Blast, Royal Match, Candy Crush) earn $1M+/month each

### 2.2 Market Gap

The puzzle genre is dominated by:
- Match-3 (Candy Crush, Royal Match)
- Block blast
- Color sort / water sort
- Screw jam / parking jam

**No major puzzle game uses board folding.** This is a white space in a proven, high-revenue category.

### 2.3 Comparable Performance

| Game | Downloads (Year 1) | Year 1 Revenue |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| Monument Valley | ~14M | ~$10M |
| Threes! | ~3M | ~$2M |
| Alto's Odyssey | ~10M | ~$4M |
| Small puzzle indie | ~50K | ~$35K |

Foldlight targets the indie tier initially, with upside to mid-tier if Apple features.

## 3. Product Strategy

### 3.1 Differentiation

| Feature | Foldlight | Competitors |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Mechanic | Board folding (unique) | Matching, sorting, clearing |
| Content | Procedurally infinite | Hand-crafted (limited) or formulaic |
| Aesthetic | Premium, magical, cozy | Often generic |
| Monetization | Player-first, no energy | Energy timers, aggressive IAP |
| Offline | 100% local | Often server-dependent |

### 3.2 Competitive Moat

1. **Mechanic patent potential** — folding mechanic is novel and defensible
2. **Procedural generation IP** — the reverse-construction algorithm is proprietary
3. **Brand identity** — "Foldlight" as a distinctive premium puzzle brand
4. **Content library** — grows continuously with no incremental content cost

### 3.3 Product Roadmap

**v1.0 (Launch):** Core fold mechanic, 10 biomes, infinite mode, daily puzzle, 8 IAP products
**v1.1 (Month 2):** Challenge Pass subscription, seasonal events, skin bundles
**v1.2 (Month 4):** GameKit achievements, leaderboards for daily puzzle
**v1.3 (Month 6):** iCloud sync, iPad optimization
**v2.0 (Month 9):** Diagonal folds, new tile types, expanded world restoration story

## 4. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 4.1 Launch Channels

**App Store Optimization (ASO)**
- Primary keywords: "puzzle game," "folding puzzle," "infinite puzzle," "light puzzle"
- Compelling preview video showing fold mechanic
- Screenshots showcasing biome art and world restoration
- Localized metadata (EN-US, EN-GB, EN-AU)

**Organic / Social**
- TikTok: short satisfying fold-solve videos (high virality potential)
- Instagram Reels: beautiful aesthetic content
- Reddit: r/iosgaming, r/puzzlegames launch posts
- Twitter/X: development diary, launch announcement

**Press**
- TouchArcade, PocketGamer, AppAdvice review submissions
- Indie game journalists briefings 4 weeks pre-launch

**Apple Relations**
- Submit for App Store feature consideration 6 weeks pre-launch
- Ensure compliance with all Apple guidelines
- Use Apple's latest frameworks (SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit 2) for feature eligibility

### 4.2 Launch Timeline

| Week | Action |

## 7. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|-----------|
| App Store rejection | High | Low | Follow Apple guidelines strictly, pre-review checklist |
| No Apple feature | Medium | Medium | Strong ASO + TikTok organic strategy |
| Low Day-1 retention | High | Medium | Strong tutorial, satisfying core loop, onboarding A/B test |
| Mechanic is too hard | High | Low | Gradual difficulty curve, generous undo, clear tutorial |
| Copycat emerges | Medium | Low | Speed to market, brand identity, continuous feature development |
| Low conversion rate | Medium | Medium | Multiple price points, A/B test bundle offer timing |
