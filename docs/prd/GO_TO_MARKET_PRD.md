# Go-to-Market PRD — Foldlight

**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Date:** 2026-06-27 | **Status:** Draft

## Overview
Platform: iOS App Store | Launch: Q4 2026 | Markets: US, UK, CA, AU

## Target Audience
Primary: Casual puzzle players 25-40 who enjoy Monument Valley, Alto's Odyssey style games. 10-20 min sessions, 2x/day. Motivated by relaxation + mental engagement.

## Positioning
"For casual puzzle players tired of match-3 and color sort, Foldlight lets you fold the board itself — genuinely unlike anything on the App Store."

## Core Messages
- Headline: "Fold the board. Bend the rules. Restore the world."
- First board-folding puzzle — completely original mechanic
- Infinite levels — procedurally generated forever
- Beautiful premium aesthetic, free to download
- No energy, no timers, play at your pace
